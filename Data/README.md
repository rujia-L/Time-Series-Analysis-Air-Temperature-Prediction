## Data

The dataset is stored in `projectData24.mat` and contains several years of hourly measurements.

### Column Definitions

| Column | Variable | Description | Unit |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | `year` | Year | — |
| 2 | `month` | Month | — |
| 3 | `day` | Day of month | — |
| 4 | `hour` | Hour of day | — |
| 5 | `rsw` | Reflected shortwave radiation | W/m² |
| 6 | `rn` | Net radiation | W/m² |
| 7 | `ghf` | Earth heat flux | W/m² |
| 8 | `ta` | Air temperature | °C |

### Notes
- Time is recorded at **hourly** resolution.
- Radiation and heat flux variables are measured in **W/m²**, and air temperature is measured in **°C**.
